{#Services#}

Services

Security

We offer maximum security in your files. If you are new here, first you need to register then you can login with the credentials and only you with your account can access the files that you upload, unless you want to share with someone else.

Protection

As you guess, you don't have to worry for protection in a cloud service. Also for a deleted file you have 30 days to get it back if you want, you just have to contact us. After those days it will be deleted permanently.

Account Settings

After you create your account, when logged in, you can change your account settings like password, avatar or even delete your account permanently, but we know, you don't like that either, do you?

Directories

You can create new directories or delete existing ones and put different files inside different directories. So you can manage the structure as you like. When you delete a directory you will delete the files inside.

Upload Files

What use would our web application have if you didn't have the service to upload. You can upload files like images, documents, etc. Also you can open uploaded files inside our application if you would like that.

Download Files

Every file related with your account can be downloaded to your machine. Downloading may be for different reasons, for example you want the same file to be downloaded in many machines, so why use a USB when you have us?

Share Files

You can share your files with who ever you like you just need to know his email address and of course he should have an account here. When you delete your file that file will be deleted for everyone not just you.

Delete Files

This service has been mentioned before and yes you will be able to delete every file you don't want anymore. After pressing the delete button you will be asked that if you are sure. If you want your file back contact us not longer than 30 days.

Free Use

This maybe is not even called a service but it's the best of all. All of the services provided by us are free. You will not have to pay when you open your account or when you add files or never. We believe you like that.
